Homtel Privacy Statement

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information we collect

We collect the information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, phone number, profile picture, booking details, food orders, feedback messages, and any preferences you set in the app. We also receive technical information such as device type, operating system version, unique user ID, and basic usage analytics.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to: manage your room, venue, equipment, and food & drinks bookings; maintain your account and loyalty points; provide customer support; improve our services and user experience; and comply with legal obligations.

3. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with: (a) service providers who help us operate the app (such as cloud hosting, authentication, and analytics providers), and (b) hotel staff who need access to fulfill your bookings and service requests. All such parties are required to protect your data and use it only for the agreed purposes.

4. Third-party services

We use Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Cloud Storage) to power account sign-in and data storage. When you use Homtel, certain information (such as your email, user ID, and device identifiers) is processed by Firebase on Google's servers. Google's use of your data is governed by its own privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with services and comply with applicable laws. You can delete your account at any time from Settings > Account Management, which removes your profile, bookings, and other associated data.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You can exercise many of these rights directly in the app (for example by updating your profile or deleting your account), or by contacting us using the details below.

7. Children's privacy

Homtel is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. International data transfers

Homtel is operated from the United States. If you access the app from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the U.S. or other countries where our service providers operate. By using Homtel you consent to such transfers.

9. Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and access controls. However, no system is completely secure, so please use a strong, unique password and keep your device secure.

10. Changes to this statement

We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you in the app and update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Homtel after the changes take effect means you accept the updated statement.

11. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Statement or how your data is handled, please contact our support team via the Contact Us section in the Profile tab, or email us at homtelsupport@gmail.com.
